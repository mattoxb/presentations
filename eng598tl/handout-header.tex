Manager or Reflector: Consider the objectives of this activity and your team's
experience with it, and then answer the following questions after consulting
with your team.

\begin{enumerate}
	\item What was a \textbf{strength} of this activity?  List one aspect that helped it
  achieve its purpose.

  \vfil

\item What is one things we could do to \textbf{improve} this activity to make it more effective?

  \vfil

\item What \textbf{insights} did you have about the activity, either the content or at the meta level?
 
\end{enumerate}
